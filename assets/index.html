Product Lane

  • Rust packages with native binaries — the supported, tested product surface.
  • Go, TypeScript, S-expression targets — emit-only research protototypes; not a supported runtime.
  • Wasm, LLVM, Metal, WGSL — host-backend compiler lanes.

External Projects
